These terms cover how subscriptions, billing, and refunds work when you use
**DINGG** — the all-in-one salon, spa, and beauty clinic booking software. By
creating an account or using the software, you agree to the terms below. They
help us keep the service dependable so managing your business stays effortless
and enjoyable.

## Your subscription

DINGG is provided as a subscription to the plan you select. Explore
[all DINGG booking software](/services) to choose the plan that fits your
business, whether you run a single chair or a multi-location group. Your
subscription gives you and your team access to the booking, membership, and
reporting tools included in that plan for the period you've paid for.

## Billing

Subscription fees are billed in advance for each billing period. Charges
recur automatically at the start of every period until you cancel. You're
responsible for keeping your billing details current so your service stays
uninterrupted. Any applicable taxes are added to your invoice.

## Cancellation

You can cancel your DINGG subscription at any time from your account. When you
cancel, your plan stays active through the end of the current billing period and
won't renew after that. We don't lock you into long contracts — the goal is
software you keep using because it works.

## Refunds

Subscription fees already billed for the current period are generally
non-refundable, since access remains available through the period you've paid
for. If something isn't working as it should, tell us within 7 days and we'll
look into it and make it right. Prepaid annual plans follow the refund terms
shared at the time of purchase.

## Acceptable use

DINGG is built to run your salon, spa, or beauty clinic operations. Please use
the software lawfully, keep your account credentials secure, and don't attempt
to disrupt the service for other businesses. You're responsible for the
accuracy of the booking, client, and payment data you enter.

## Service availability

We work hard to keep DINGG dependable so your bookings and memberships run
smoothly. From time to time we may schedule maintenance or release updates to
improve the software. We'll aim to keep any disruption brief and, where we can,
let you know in advance.

## Your data

The client lists, appointments, and reports you create in DINGG belong to your
business. We handle that information in line with our [privacy policy](/privacy),
and you can export your data when you need it.

## Contact

Questions about these terms or your subscription? Reach the DINGG team and
we'll help — [book your appointment now](/contact) for a walkthrough and we'll
get your salon, spa, or clinic set up on DINGG.
