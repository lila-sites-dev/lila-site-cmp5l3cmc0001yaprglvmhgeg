We're DINGG, and we build software that makes running a salon, spa, or beauty
clinic feel less like juggling and more like getting on with the work you love.
From bookings and memberships to reports and reminders, DINGG brings the moving
parts of a busy business into one calm, easy-to-use place.

We started DINGG with a simple idea: the tools you use every day should be
comprehensive without being complicated. So whether you run a single chair or a
multi-location group, DINGG is designed to streamline operations and make
management genuinely enjoyable — clear, friendly software that gets out of your
way and lets you focus on your clients.

DINGG is all-in-one by design. It supports a wide range of businesses, from
salons and barbershops to nail salons, pet salons, spas, wellness centers,
beauty clinics, skin clinics, tattoo studios, and gyms. To explore what fits
your business, browse [all DINGG booking software](/services), take a closer look
at our [salon booking software](/services/salon-booking-software) and
[spa booking software](/services/spa-booking-software), then
[book your appointment now](/contact) and we'll show you how DINGG works for you.
