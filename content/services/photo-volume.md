## Don't let an under-photographed profile cost you bookings

Most clients decide whether to book before they ever speak to you. They scroll your profile, look for photos of real work, and judge in seconds. A thin, under-photographed profile gives them nothing to go on — so they keep scrolling to a salon that shows its work.

Building photo volume is one of the simplest ways to close that gap. A profile with a deep, current set of photos shows the range and quality of what you do, and turns browsers into bookings.

DINGG gives salons, spas, and beauty clinics comprehensive, all-in-one software that streamlines day-to-day management, so keeping your profile and your operations in shape stays effortless. While DINGG runs the bookings, memberships, and reporting behind the scenes, your photo-rich profile does the convincing out front.

### How to build photo volume that converts

- Capture finished work consistently — a quick photo after each standout service adds up fast.
- Cover your full menu so clients see every reason to book, not just one.
- Keep lighting clean and backgrounds simple so the work, not the clutter, is the focus.
- Refresh regularly; a profile that's clearly active reads as a salon that's in demand.
