## Your day ends at 8pm. Your clients' planning doesn't.

Plenty of people only get a moment to think about their next salon or spa visit
late in the evening — after work, after dinner, once the house is quiet. If your
only way to book is a phone line that goes dark at closing time, those requests
wait until morning, and some never come back.

DINGG is salon, spa, and beauty clinic software built to keep that door open.
Your booking page runs all night, so a client deciding at 11pm can pick a service,
choose a time, and confirm — without waiting for you to reopen. Every late-night
booking lands in the same all-in-one dashboard you use to manage the rest of your
operations, making the whole thing effortless to stay on top of.

Built for the Mumbai spa and wellness scene, DINGG supports a wide range of
businesses — from salons and barbershops to nail salons, spas, wellness centres,
and beauty clinics — so after-hours booking works the same way whatever you run.
